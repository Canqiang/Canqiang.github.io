My research record connects biomedical questions with reproducible data analysis and machine learning. It includes three peer-reviewed papers and patent work developed alongside research and engineering teams.

## Publications

### Anti-PD-1 response

I am a co-first author of the 2021 *npj Genomic Medicine* paper “Ratio of the interferon-γ signature to the immunosuppression signature predicts anti-PD-1 therapy response in melanoma.” My contribution included data processing and machine-learning analysis.

### Metagenomic geolocation

I am a co-first author of the 2020 *Biology Direct* paper “A machine learning framework to determine geolocations from metagenomic profiling.” The work combined microbial abundance profiles, feature selection, classification, and geographic reasoning.

### ImmuneCells.Sig evaluation

I am a co-author of the 2021 *Nature Communications* paper “Inconsistent prediction capability of ImmuneCells.Sig across different RNA-seq datasets.” The study examined how a published predictive signature generalized across independent datasets.

## Patent work

The reviewed record also includes patent applications for metagenomic geolocation (`CN110827915A`), a GMM-based Stereo-seq data-bias correction method (`WO2024000313A1` / `PCT/CN2022/102505`), and a multi-modal transfer-learning framework for predicting the efficacy of tumor immune checkpoint inhibitors (`CN112634985A`).
