My medical-AI work spans image models, biomedical data analysis, and research engineering. The projects moved between pathology, treatment-response research, and spatial omics while keeping the biological question central to the modeling approach.

## Pathology vision

I developed H&E cell-segmentation and classification pipelines using U-Net and convolutional feature extractors including ResNet, DenseNet, and Siamese networks.

I later led research on whole-slide-image methods for stratifying prognostic recurrence risk, connecting model design with the clinical research objective.

## Translational data work

My biomedical data work included a machine-learning framework for locating metagenomic samples and expression-signature analysis for predicting anti-PD-1 treatment response. These projects contributed to peer-reviewed publications and patent work.

## Spatial omics

At BGI Research, I led spatial clinical algorithm work, developed semantic segmentation for ssDNA-stained tissue images, and worked on GMM-based correction of Stereo-seq expression data using spatial and expression information.

## Scope

This page summarizes selected technical responsibilities and reviewed project results. It does not make claims about clinical deployment or patient outcomes beyond the cited research record.
